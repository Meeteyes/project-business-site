How can I find the schedule of screenings?

You can find our full live events schedule in our program as well as on our interactive event grid PDF.

When can I start watching?

Most films and programs will be available from 12:01am on April 8, 2022 until 11:59pm PT on April 18, 2022. Select events and films have set viewing times and dates noted within the digital program and on the website.

How long do I have to watch a film that is not a timed event?

For films that are not timed events, passholders can watch the film until the set number of views are reached.

Can I watch the films from anywhere?

Film viewing is limited to the EU. All items are geo-blocked so you won't be able to access them outside the EU

What happens if I forget my password?

Use the password reset option on the login page to reset your password. You will need access to the email address you originally used to log in.

How do I access Works-In-Progress?
